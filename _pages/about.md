Dr. Jiahao Wang is currently a research engineer in **AI safety and governance** at Huawei Technologies Co., Ltd. He received his PhD and B.S. degrees in Computer Science from Beihang University in 2022 and 2017 respectively. He also worked as a research intern in Meituan in 2021.
